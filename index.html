The Engines of Exponential Growth

This hyper-exponential curve is fueled by three self-reinforcing cycles, creating a potential "industrial explosion."

Algorithmic Loop

AI models improve and accelerate the development of new, more sophisticated algorithms with minimal human intervention.

Chip Loop

AI designs the next generation of semiconductors, creating a cycle where better chips enable faster AI, which designs even better chips.

Industrial Loop

AI and robotics advance to build and operate factories, producing the very hardware needed to fuel the other two loops.
